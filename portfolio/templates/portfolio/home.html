About Me

Dr. N. Nagabhooshanam, an accomplished academic and researcher, holds a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering with expertise in interfacial heat transfer in die-casting processes. He currently serves as an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Aditya Engineering College, India.


With over a decade of teaching and research experience, his work spans diverse fields, including fluid mechanics, material sciences, and advanced manufacturing. He holds several patents for AI-powered devices, renewable energy solutions, and advanced composites.


He is the Founder and CEO of the Cosmas Institute of Research and Technology , focusing on fostering innovation and education.
